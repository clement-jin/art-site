Welcome!

Girl with the Pearl Earring

The world of art is vast and rich, with masterpieces from a plethora of styles and genres. This site is designed to guide you through the journey of art through time, exploring the works of pivotal artists from the beginning of art right up to the modern day. If you are a beginner to art, do not fear! This site will explore these works in a clear and beginner-friendly way. By the end of your journey here, you will have a deeper understanding of how art came to be what it is.

Renaissance

The Mona Lisa: Da Vinci

One of the most famous pieces of art in the world, forged in the Renaissance period. Worked on by Da Vinci for 4 years, and residing in the Louvre, Paris, this is one of the icons of art - A great starting point.

Modern

Still Life: Lichtenstein

A peice of beautiful modern art by American artist Roy Lichtenstein shows us that modern art can be just as expressive and intereseting as other genres.

Impressionism

Impression, sunrise: Monet

A personal favourite of mine. The impressionists challenged the very foundations of art, painting what they felt instead of trying to replicate exactly what they saw. Perhaps this is particularly important for painting in an age of cameras?

Romanticism

Wanderer above the Sea of Fog : Friedrich

Forged in the 18th century, the Romanticist movement focused on the idealisation of nature and the glorification of the past. In fact, this movement expressed suspicion to science and industrailisation, arising in part as a reaction to the rapid indudstrial revolution.
